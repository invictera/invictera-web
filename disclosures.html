Algorithmic Tracing

Our applications utilize deterministic mathematical models—including Spaced Repetition algorithms and Bayesian Knowledge Tracing approximations—to calculate knowledge decay and forecast mastery probabilities dynamically. The "Predictive Readiness Score" and "Knowledge Decay" metrics are algorithmic estimates based on in-app performance and should not be taken as a guarantee of exam passage.
