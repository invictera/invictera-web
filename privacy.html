Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026  ·  Invictera LLC

Scope of this document. This Privacy Policy applies exclusively to Invictera's mobile applications. It does not govern the collection or processing of information submitted through invictera.com or through any advisory engagement with Invictera LLC.

1. Local-First Processing & Data Architecture

Invictera's mobile applications are engineered with a strict, privacy-first architecture. Our advanced learning algorithms—including the Knowledge Decay Engine and Bayesian Knowledge Tracing models—process your performance telemetry locally on your device.

We use Apple's CloudKit to securely sync your professional study progress (quiz scores, flashcard states, and mastery probabilities) across your devices.

  • Anonymous Identity: We link your iCloud User Record ID with RevenueCat to provide a seamless, account-free subscription experience.
  • Zero PII: We do not collect, request, or store your name, email address, physical location, or biometric data on our servers.
  • Local Storage: All synced study data stays within your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's enterprise security practices.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

We strictly limit our third-party integrations to the following essential partners:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
RevenueCat Subscription status and entitlement management Anonymous app-user identifier only
PostHog Anonymous product telemetry, crash reports, and usage diagnostics (session length, feature usage) No personally identifiable information
Apple Secure payment processing and CloudKit data hosting Governed by Apple's privacy policy

No Selling of Data. No data of any kind is sold, rented, or shared with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing firms.

3. Your Rights & Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Deletion: Permanently delete your data at any time by clearing your iCloud storage for the app or by deleting the app itself.
  • Resetting Progress: Resetting progress within the app clears your local device cache. To completely purge data from the cloud, navigate to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.
  • Subscriptions: For billing inquiries, cancellations, or refunds, contact Apple Support directly. All financial transactions are handled exclusively by the App Store. Invictera LLC does not have access to or control over App Store transactions.

4. GDPR, CCPA & Applicable Rights

Given our zero-PII architecture, most data subject rights requests will confirm that Invictera LLC holds no personally identifiable information about you. However, if you wish to submit a formal data request under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable privacy law, please contact us at hello@invictera.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

5. Children's Privacy

Our applications are rated 4+ on the Apple App Store and are accessible to users of all ages. However, they are designed for professionals preparing for advanced certifications and licensure examinations and are not intended for use by children. Because our architecture collects zero personally identifiable information (PII), no personal data of any user — regardless of age — is collected or stored on our servers. All study data remains within the user's private Apple iCloud account.

In the event of any conflict between this Privacy Policy and Apple's EULA with respect to usage of our applications on Apple devices, Apple's EULA shall govern matters relating to the App Store, in-app purchases, and platform-level terms.

Apple Inc. is a third-party beneficiary of Invictera's Terms of Service as they apply to iOS applications, and Apple has the right to enforce those terms against you as a third-party beneficiary. This Privacy Policy addresses data practices specific to Invictera LLC and supplements — but does not replace — Apple's own privacy framework.

Apple's Privacy Policy is available at apple.com/legal/privacy.
